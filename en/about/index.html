About

A Clan cats' name generator based on Erin Hunter's Warriors book series.

It will randomly generate a name in the format of Prefixsuffix, where all prefixes and suffixes comes from cats of the official Warriors universe. Data is collected from Crystal Pool and will be updated periodically.
